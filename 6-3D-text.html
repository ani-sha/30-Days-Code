The text is given multiple shadows adding 1px to the right and 1 px to downwards every time to give it the depth effect. To outline the text, similar technique is used. 4 shadows are applied, 1px right, 1px left, 1px up, 1 px down. But, the outline for the corners of the text is mixing, so we apply, four more shadows towards 1 px 1px, 1px -1px, -1px 1px and -1px -1px for the 4 corners.
